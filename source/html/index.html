Our Mission

Rescue

Our aim is to rescue abandoned and unwanted pets and place them into permanent, loving homes. Champaign County Pet Rescue is a no-kill shelter. We keep our pets until we have found them their forever homes.

Spay & Neuter

We spay and neuter all of our animals which helps reduce the population of stray animals that can devastate local ecosystems and kill off populations of birds.

Adopt

All of our animals are waiting to go to their forever homes. There is a flat fee of $100 to adopt. No animal is unwanted. We hope that you can help make their dreams a reality.

Meet the very handsome and FUN loving Storm! Storm is a 2 year old solid gray boy with a fluffy tail. He has big green eyes and is absolutely adorable. Storm is a very friendly and sociable guy. He wants to be your friend instantly. He arrived from Louisiana in a transport van with other cats and dogs. And has really adapted very quickly to the shelter. He is still in his "kitty condo" until he acclimates more to the shelter and if you walk by he sticks his paw out to grab you. We are going to say no young children for him because he does like to play a little rough sometimes. He has a lot of personality and SASS! And he loves to play, he was even entertaining himself. Storm is a fun cat that we think likes to be the center of attention.
