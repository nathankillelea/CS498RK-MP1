Our Mission

Rescue

Our aim is to rescue abandoned and unwanted pets and place them into permanent, loving homes. Champaign County Pet Rescue is a no-kill shelter. We keep our pets until we have found them their forever homes.

Spay & Neuter

We spay and neuter all of our animals which helps reduce the population of stray animals that can devastate local ecosystems and kill off populations of birds.

Adopt

All of our animals are waiting to go to their forever homes. There is a flat fee of $100 to adopt. No animal is unwanted. We hope that you can help make their dreams a reality.
